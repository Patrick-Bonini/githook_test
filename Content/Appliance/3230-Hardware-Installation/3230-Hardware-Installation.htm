Solace PubSub+ 3230 Installation Steps

This section describes how to install and power up the Solace PubSub+ 3230.

Only allow trained and qualified service personnel to install the Solace PubSub+ 3230. Removal of equipment covers will void warranty.

Ensure that you thoroughly read and understand the information and safety guidelines contained in Preparing Your Site before you begin.

Rack-Mount Requirements

A Solace PubSub+ 3230 must be installed in a four-post 19 in. rack or cabinet. Four‑post mounting is required due to the weight of the appliance.

One rack kit is supplied with a Solace PubSub+ 3230 in the shipping crate. This rack kit is required to install the appliance in a four-post 19 in. rack. Each rack kit contains two inner rails and two slide assemblies that can adjust to fit vertical rail racks from 22.5 to 38 in. deep.

Rack Kit Contents

After you have ensured that the rack requirements have been met, consider the following requirements before installing or removing a Solace PubSub+ 3230:

  • There must be at least 2.5 feet (76.2 cm) of free space both in front of and behind the appliance.
  • To maintain the stability of the rack and reduce the chance of it tipping over:
    • Ensure that the rack is level and stable before extending an appliance from the rack.
    • When mounting equipment in the rack, load it from the bottom to the top with the heaviest units at the bottom of the rack. When possible, load the heaviest unit in the rack first.
    • When removing equipment from a rack, remove equipment from the top to the bottom.
  • Use caution when pressing the rail release latches and sliding an appliance into or out of a rack; the slide rails can pinch your fingers.

Tool and Equipment Requirements

The following tools and equipment are required when installing a Solace PubSub+ 3230:

  • Standard protective equipment such as eye protection, work gloves, and protective footwear
  • No. 2 Phillips screwdriver
  • Small insulated adjustable wrench
