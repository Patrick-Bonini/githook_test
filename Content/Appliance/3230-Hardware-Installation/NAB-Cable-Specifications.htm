NAB Port and Cable Specifications

The NAB-0401ET supports up to four GigE interfaces through RJ-45 connectors only. It occupies one slot.

Faceplate LEDs

As shown in below, a NAB-0401ET has two LEDs on each of the four RJ-45 Ethernet ports to indicate their link and activity status:

  • the green link LED indicates network connectivity when on
  • the yellow activity LED indicates receive activity when blinking

Electrical GigE Wiring Specifications

The NAB-0401ET has four RJ-45 ports. Connect to these ports with CAT‑5e unshielded twisted pair cable. Cross-over or straight‑through Ethernet cables are supported.

In all cases cable length should not exceed 330 feet (100 meters).
