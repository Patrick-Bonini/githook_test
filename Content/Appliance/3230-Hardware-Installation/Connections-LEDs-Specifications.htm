The two recessed buttons located to the right of the LEDs provide the mechanisms for manually resetting the appliance.

  • The () On/Standby button can be used to toggle appliance power on or off.
  • The RST button can be used to immediately reset and reboot the appliance without the cooperation of any software or operating system on the appliance.

These buttons can be activated with a paper clip.

Do not use the On/Standby button or the RST button unless necessary. Both the On/Standby and RST buttons cause a disruption to service because they restart the appliance.

Solace PubSub+ 3230 Technical Specifications

Technical Specifications for Solace PubSub+ 3230s

Weight

40 lb. (18.1 kg)

Dimensions

2U rack-mountable chassis

Height 3.5 x Width 17 x Depth 23 inches

(89 x 431.8 x 584 mm)

Environmental Requirements
  • Operating Temperature: 50° to 104° F (10 to 40° C)
  • Operating Humidity: 5% to 85% (non-condensing)
  • Storage Temperature: –40° to 150° F (–40° to 65° C),
    95% relative humidity
  • Storage Humidity: 5% to 95% (non-conducting)

Space Requirements
  • 3 feet (91 cm) behind appliance or rack
  • No space requirements for sides of units or rack
  • Do not block air vents on front or back of the appliance
  • Position appliance with easy access to connections it needs for power, local communications, and remote communications

Airflow Direction

In through the front and out through the back.

Power

Input Rating: 100-240VAC, 8-4A, 47-63 Hz

Typical Consumption: 250W, 275VA @120V

Heat Load: 900BTU/hr
