Tool and Equipment Requirements

  • standard protective equipment such as eye protection, work gloves, and protective footwear
  • scissors (for cutting nylon straps)
  • No. 2 Phillips screwdriver
  • masking tape or a felt-tip pen (for use in marking the rack mounting holes to be used)
  • adjustable pliers or wrench
  • tape measure
  • observation of proper ESD handling techniques and use of personal grounding devices when working on shelves, cables connected to blades, and blades
