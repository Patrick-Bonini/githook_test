3560 Appliance Installation

This section describes how to install and power up a Solace PubSub+ 3560 in a four-post 19 in. (482.6 mm) wide rack or cabinet that meets the specifications and guidelines indicated in Rack Requirements.

Only allow trained and qualified service personnel to install Solace PubSub+ 3560s.

Tool and Equipment Requirements

The following tools and equipment are required when installing a Solace PubSub+ 3560:

  • Standard protective equipment such as eye protection, work gloves, and protective footwear
  • Observation of proper ESD handling techniques and use of personal grounding devices when working on shelves, cables connected to blades, and blades
  • Phillips No. 2 screwdriver
