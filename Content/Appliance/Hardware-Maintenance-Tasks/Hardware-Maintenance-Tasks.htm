Hardware Maintenance

This section provides hardware maintenance procedures for Solace PubSub+ 3230s, 3260s, 3530s, and 3560s.

For clarity, the illustrations in this section show the appliance removed from the equipment rack or cabinet, and all cords and cables are detached. However, unless otherwise instructed in a procedure, do not remove the appliance from the equipment rack or cabinet, and do not disconnect any cords or cables.
