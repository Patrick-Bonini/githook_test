On / Standby Button

The physical () on/standby button located on Solace PubSub+ appliances can be used to toggle appliance power on or to a low power state. The button can be activated without the cooperation of any software or operating system on the appliance.

The location of the button depends on the appliance model:

  • Solace PubSub+ 3230/3260: The on/standby button is located below the LEDs on the front panel of a PubSub+ 3230 and PubSub+ 3260. Use a straightened paper clip to activate this recessed button.
  • Solace PubSub+ 3530/3560: The on/standby button is located on the rear panel of a PubSub+ 3530 and PubSub+ 3560. This button can be pressed with your finger.

Power On

To power on an appliance, press the on/standby button.

Power Off

To manually turn off power to the appliance, hold the on/standby until the system powers down (this typically takes 10 seconds or less).

Do not use the on/standby button unless necessary because it restarts the event broker, which will cause a disruption to service. However, if an appliance fails to respond for an extended time period, use of this button may be necessary to correct the issue.
