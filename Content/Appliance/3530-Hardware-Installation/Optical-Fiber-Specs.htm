Optical Fibre Specs

The TC 86 series of subordinate specifications for media characteristics are IEC 60793-2 (most notably -10 and -50).

EC 60793-2-10/50 are referenced in ISO/IEC 11801, which defines performance categories. ISO/IEC 11801 performance categories are most commonly referenced in various application standards such as IEEE-802.3 (Ethernet) and ANSI/INCITS-T11 FC (Fibre Channel).

In North America, TIA/EIA has parallel specifications for fibre characteristics referenced in the TIA 568B Structured Cabling Standard.

Optical Loss Budget and Operating Distances

Optical loss budgets and operating distances are provided for the following hardware components:

  • ADB Mate Link: Optical Loss Budget and Operating Distance
  • Fibre Channel HBA: Optical Loss Budget and Operating Distance
  • All Ethernet standards defined in IEEE-802.3-2008 and associated annexes.
  • Loss budget includes all losses in the fibre media including channel losses, splices, and connectors.
