Configuring Client Authorization

After clients connecting to a Message VPN are authenticated, they must be authorized to access event broker resources and messaging capabilities. How much access that a client is authorized for is determined by the client profile and ACL profile it is assigned.

Client profiles and ACL profiles can be assigned to clients based on the client usernames that they use (refer to Configuring Client Usernames) or on an LDAP authorization group that they belong to (refer to Configuring LDAP Authorization Groups).

Clients' access to endpoints is also determined by permission levels that are configured for each individual endpoint.
