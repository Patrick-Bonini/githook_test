Central Monitoring Service and Datadog Agents

Operational monitoring metrics, statistics, and logs are centrally monitored from the . Microservices that are part of the communicate with the central monitoring service to retrieve reports and data regarding the s. The central monitoring service is Datadog. Datadog is a a third-party service that provides a secure solution built on a highly reliable stack for collecting and storing information that's SOC2, Type I and SOC2, Type II compliant. The SOC2 reports are available on the Datadog website.

Datadog agents collect only system-level information and metrics required to monitor and manage the s. The system-level information and metrics do not contain any personal-identifiable information – only monitoring data about the s. The information exchanged between the Datadog agents and the central monitoring service is encrypted using TLS 1.2. For more information about the data collected, see Summary of Log Information Collected.
