VPC/VNet Isolation

You can deploy your s in a Kubernetes cluster in an isolated Virtual Private Cloud or Virtual Network (VPC/VNet). An isolated VPC/VNet is recommended when you need additional security and want to keep your s entirely separated from other enterprise applications/services and environments to limit risks and the vectors of attack.
