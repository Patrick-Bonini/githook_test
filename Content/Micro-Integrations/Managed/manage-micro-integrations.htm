Viewing Metrics

Metrics appear at the top of the Details page and provide information about performance and usage, including:

  • the state of the

  • message and byte usage

  • total messages and errors

We collect metric data at a high frequency and you can see up-to-the minute data by refreshing the browser tab. You can view metrics to ensure that your is running, and that the messaging and data speeds are meeting your expectations. The table below explains the metrics shown on the card:

Metric Definition
State

The current status of the . The status for a can be:

  • Not Deployed—The has not been deployed.

  • Unable to deploy—The cannot be deployed. There may be configuration problems. Review the .

  • Deploying—The is being deployed.

  • Running—The is deployed and active.
Messages Per Second The amount of messages being handled by the per second.
Bytes Per Second The amount of bytes being handled by the per second.
Total Messages The total amount of messages handled since the was deployed.
Total Errors The total amount of errors that have occurred since the was deployed.
Last Updated The time that the metrics were last updated.
Update Interval The frequency in minutes that we collect metric data.
